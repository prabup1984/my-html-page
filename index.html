About Me

I am an enthusiastic academician specializing in Geography, with a strong focus on Hydrological Modeling using Remote Sensing and GIS.

I completed my Ph.D. in Geography from Tamil University, Thanjavur, with a dissertation titled "Hydrological Modeling of Upper Vaigai Subbasin, Tamil Nadu, India Using Remote Sensing and GIS."

I bring valuable experience in both higher education and school-level teaching, having served as an Assistant Professor at Nirmala College for Women, Coimbatore, and currently working as a Graduate Teacher in Geography with the Department of School Education, Government of Tamil Nadu, in The Nilgiris District, since September 2014.
